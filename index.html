Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

How long does it take to receive the photos?

We usually take as little as 30 minutes to edit and process your photos. This time may vary depending on the number of photos and complexity of editing requested.

Can I choose my own shooting location?

Yes of course! You can choose the shooting location you want. We can also provide location recommendations that suit your theme and needs.

Are there additional fees for out of town photo shoots?

Yes, we charge an additional fee for out of town shoots to cover transportation and accommodation costs. This fee will be adjusted to the location and distance of travel. Please contact us to discuss further details.

Do you provide make-up and styling services?

No, but we work with several make-up professionals and stylists who you can hire for a photo shoot. Please contact us for further information and available packages.

Can I get all photo files in digital format?

Yes, all photos taken during the photo shoot will be sent to you in digital format. We provide the photos via cloud drive for easy access and download.

Do you offer special shooting packages for certain events?

We offer various photo shoot packages for various types of events such as weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and others. This package can be tailored to your needs and budget. Please contact us to discuss further details.
